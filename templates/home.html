A Beginners Guide to All Things Backpacking

Welcome to Tim's Trails! Whether you're a brand new backpacker, or someone looking for new trip ideas, I'm glad your here. The purpose of Tim's Trails is to provide a simple, no-frills approach to backpacking. You see, the internet is saturated with advice, how-tos, and guides on backpacking. Everyone has different likes and dislikes, and it’s hard to know what information to trust, especially when you’re brand new. I'm hoping this is where I can help... Like you might be, I was once a novice backpacker. I didn't take a course, I had no one to show me the ropes. I learned by making mistakes. Now I've come up with what I think are the essentials - It's what you need to get started.
